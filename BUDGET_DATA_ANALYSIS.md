## üéì Understanding Union Budget Terms

### **Revenue Expenditure vs Capital Expenditure**
- **Revenue**: Day-to-day expenses (salaries, pensions, subsidies, interest)
- **Capital**: Asset creation (roads, railways, buildings, infrastructure)

### **Fiscal Deficit**
- Gap between total expenditure and total revenue (excluding borrowings)
- Shows how much government needs to borrow

### **Devolution to States**
- Based on Finance Commission recommendations
- 41% of divisible pool of taxes goes to states
- Additional grants for specific schemes
